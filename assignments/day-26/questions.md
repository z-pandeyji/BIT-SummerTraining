# Day 26 Assignment

## Topic: End-to-End AI Capstone With Evaluation and Deployment Plan

Complete the assignment in one folder named `solution` containing a working prototype, evidence, and a capstone report.

This is a hard-level research assignment. You are expected to combine multiple AI concepts, make engineering trade-offs, and justify your design with evidence. The assignment has only one question.

You may use any suitable Python AI, ML, NLP, or data stack already covered in class.

## Question 1: Build a Production-Ready AI Prototype Proposal

Choose one real problem and design an end-to-end AI prototype that could be useful for students, teachers, local businesses, researchers, or public information workflows. Examples include a study assistant, document question-answering tool, resume reviewer, data insight assistant, customer-support classifier, safety checker, or local-language learning aid.

Your work must include:

1. a problem statement explaining the users, workflow, and success criteria;
2. a working prototype or realistic simulation with sample inputs and outputs;
3. an architecture description covering data flow, model choice, storage, APIs or interfaces, and failure handling;
4. an evaluation plan with at least three quantitative metrics and five qualitative review criteria;
5. a test set with at least 20 examples or scenarios;
6. cost and latency estimates for local use, API use, or both;
7. security and privacy controls, including how secrets, user data, and logs should be handled;
8. a deployment and monitoring plan covering versioning, feedback, rollback, and model-quality drift;
9. a final go/no-go recommendation with evidence.

Your final report must answer this research question:

```text
Is your AI prototype ready for a limited pilot, and what evidence supports your go/no-go decision?
```

## Submission

Submit your work in one folder:

```text
assignments/day-26/submissions/your-name/solution/
```

Include a `README.md` explaining setup, usage, known limitations, and how to reproduce your evaluation.
